How is this different from "trending now" lists?+

Trending lists show what's already viral. Trend Pulse fires on early-stage signals — z-score > 2 on category baseline, creator-diversity doubling, and Google Breakout — typically 7–30 days before saturation.

Does it work for non-English creators?+

Yes — Hindi-English mix is the default. Clustering and hook extraction handle Hinglish, Tamil, Telugu, Bangla, Marathi captions.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Stripe handles cancellation; access continues until end of paid period.

Is the data private?+

Public-platform data only. Your account, watchlists, and generations are private.
